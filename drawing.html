Drawing

A self portrait piece inspired by the selfie. This drawing used charcoal and 6B pencil to blend out the shadows and structure of the face. In order to create this, I used the grid system on a photo of a selfie I took to create clear sections to work on in order to contruct the outline of the face. After the outline was finished, the shading was added gradually.
