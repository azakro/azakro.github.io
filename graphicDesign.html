Graphic Design

Using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, I worked on a few trading cards inspired from the 70s. Using colors and patterns from the disco era, the cards were modeled after the popular 'Disco 2000' set. These cards used the pen tool, shape tool, font grabbing, and blurring filters for the center photograph.
