Sculpture

Given the task to create a sculpture that uses movement and human interaction. With a group, we were able to construct a rickety clock that examplifies the insignificance of time. Time is a concept that is controlled simply by the movement of gears; by taking this model apart and making the gears on the outside and having the hands of the clock turn in an incoherant way, we are able to show its unimportance. All of the items used in the scuplture are from old and reused items.
