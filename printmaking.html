Printmaking

Created these prints with inspiration from an artificial intelligence course. Using carving blades I shaped a brain to form a sort of homage to the brain, to recognize its importance as the center of the body. The outside circles have lines scattered acroess to create a jumpled morse code, symbolizing its coded functions that the brain itself can only understand. With ink overlaying and mixing techniques, I was able to create three unique prints.
